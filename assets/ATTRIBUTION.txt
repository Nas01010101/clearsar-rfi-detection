sar_real.jpg
============
Real Copernicus Sentinel-1 C-band SAR scene (Antarctic subglacial-lake outburst /
elevation-change product). Used as an illustrative SAR backdrop on the project website.
The image does NOT depict RFI; the amber bands and cyan boxes on the site are
illustrative of the detection task only.

Source : https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Subglacial_lake_outburst_fractures_and_elevation_change_ESA511318.jpg
License : CC BY-SA 3.0 IGO
Credit  : Contains modified Copernicus Sentinel data, processed by ESA, CC BY-SA 3.0 IGO

Processing: downscaled to 1600x700 (center crop) and re-compressed for web; displayed
desaturated/darkened via CSS. No content was added or removed.
